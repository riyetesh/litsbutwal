About LITS College Butwal

Lumbini Institute of Technical Sciences (LITS) is a leading CTEVT-affiliated technical college in Butwal offering Health Sciences and Engineering programs.

Students from Rupandehi, Palpa, Gulmi, Kapilvastu and nearby districts study here with modern labs, experienced faculty, and career-focused practical training.

Programs Offered

Diploma in Pharmacy

Duration: 3 Years

Professional pharmacy training covering pharmacology, drug dispensing, hospital pharmacy practice, and community healthcare.

Lab Technician

Duration: 3 Years

Clinical laboratory training in pathology, microbiology, blood testing, and modern diagnostic techniques.

Health Assistant (HA)

Duration: 3 Years

CTEVT-affiliated general medicine diploma preparing students for hospital, clinic, and rural healthcare services.

PCL in Radiography

Duration: 3 Years

Medical imaging program covering X-ray techniques, patient safety, and diagnostic radiology practice.

PCL in Physiotherapy

Duration: 3 Years

Rehabilitation therapy training focused on injury recovery, mobility improvement, and clinical physiotherapy practice.
